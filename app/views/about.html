About Randomizer


Randomizer is a web-based app, created by Fredrik Salin, which is using chance (generates random values) in order to help you decide in a selection process you find to be hard. This might for instance be when you (and your friends perhaps) need help choosing which movie to watch. Some people might want to watch "Titanic", but you want to watch "Rambo 9". Randomizer offers a solution - Let chance decide!

Another scenario when Randomizer can be of help is for instance when somebody has to go to the local supermarket and buy snacks for the movie you just used Randomizer to select. To avoid abiased selection processes, use a third party like Randomizer instead!


Randomizer can be used for anything really. Choosing which shirt to wear for a party night, which new phone or computer to buy, or which education to pick (it might not be a good idea to use Randomizer to make all your important decisions in life though). Any decision when the alternatives have about the same weight, Randomizer can be applied. Specify your own problem and alternatives and let Randomizer help you with the decision!
